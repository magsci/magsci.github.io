If there was no atmosphere at all, the emissivity would be 1.0 and the temperature of the planet would be 254K based on this simple model. However, this uses an albedo of 0.31 which may not be the case if there was no atmosphere. Using an albedo of 0.1054 (similar to the moon) then the model gives a no-atmosphere earth a temperature of 271K. The simple atmosphere raises the temperature 17K to a steady 288K (or 15oC).
